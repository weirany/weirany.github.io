Privacy Policy — Skip Counting GO

← Back to Support

Summary

We do not collect personal data. We do not track you. We do not show ads.

Skip Counting GO is designed for children and families. The app works fully offline and processes activity on your device.

Data We Collect

  • No personal information is collected, stored, sold, or shared by the app.
  • No analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third‑party trackers are used.
  • Basic app preferences (like your selected number and mode) are saved locally on your device using system storage.

On‑Device Processing

The app uses on‑device speech synthesis to speak numbers aloud. Audio is generated on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

If future features request Microphone or Speech Recognition access, audio would only be used to enable interactive practice and would remain on‑device. We do not store or transmit audio recordings.

Purchases

Skip Counting GO offers an optional one‑time in‑app purchase to unlock additional content (6–9). Purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store using StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information.

You can restore purchases at any time using the Restore Purchases option in the app.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for children and is built to minimize data collection. Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to share or sell. If you contact us for support, we will only use your email to reply and resolve your request.
